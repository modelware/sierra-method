The purpose of this document is to formally define the high-level requirements necessary to achieve the objectives of the mission. This document establishes the baseline expectations for mission success by capturing the functional, performance, and operational requirements derived from stakeholder needs, mission goals, and proposed activities. It serves as the authoritative source for requirements that guide system design, development, integration, and verification activities throughout the mission lifecycle.

This documents applies to all systems of the mission, including ground systems, communication infrastructure, and operational procedures, to the extent that they influence mission success. The requirements presented herein are intended to be system-level and mission-critical, forming the basis for subsequent allocation to subsystems and components through systems engineering processes. This document does not include detailed design specifications or implementation-level requirements, which are addressed in downstream design documents.
